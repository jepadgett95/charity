Spice is Nice: A Plea for Flavor

Thousands of children per year living in the United States Mid-west suffer from a sever lack of flavor in their daily diet. Children whose parents say things like "Salt and pepper is all you need" or "Mayonaise is spicy" or "What is cumin?" It's a pure tragedy. We here at Spice is Nice tm aim to help educate and supply Mid-west families with spices and flavors to their everyday bland diets. For just 10 cents per month or more, you could pull an innocent child from the depths of a flavorless hell by contributing to a payload of spices, delivered directly to families in need. Please find it in your heart to have mercy on these poor children.
